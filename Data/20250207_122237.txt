Here are the main points made by Andrej Karpathy in the Tesla strategy meeting:

- The AI/FSD team has been refining the neural networks and collecting more real-world data to improve edge cases. 

- FSD Beta version 11 will soon be ready for a wider release. 

- The team is integrating more feedback from the safety team to ensure FSD is not just technologically advanced but also safe.

- Version 11 comes with a complete UI overhaul, more customization options, and significant improvements to core Autopilot functions. 

- More entertainment options have been added to the Tesla Theater.

- The software experience needs to match Tesla's hardware prowess.
